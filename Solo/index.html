Some info about Jeddah
Jeddah is a city and majorport in central Hejaz region, western Saudi Arabia. 1. It lies along the Red Sea west of Mecca. The city has a rich history dating back to the 7th century. 2. Jeddah was the port of Mecca and was thus the site where the majority of Muslim pilgrims landed while journeying to the holy cities of Mecca and Medina. 3. Jeddah is also a commercial hub and hosted 3.431 million people in 2010.
Some info about Mecca
Mecca, also known as Makkah, is a city located in the Sirat Mountains in western Saudi Arabia, inland from the Red Sea coast. It is the holiest city in Islam and the site of the Kaaba, which is the central focus of the Hajj pilgrimage and the direction of Muslims’ daily prayers. 1. Mecca is the birthplace of the Prophet Muhammad and was his home until 622 CE, when he was forced to flee to Medina. He returned and captured the city in 6304 CE.
Some info about Riadh
Riyadh is the capital and largest city of Saudi Arabia. 1. It is situated in the middle of the an-Nafud desert and was formerly known as Hajr al-Yamamah. Riyadh is a blend of the medieval and the modern, where traditional souks, interesting museums, and ancient architecture are located alongside highrises, world-class malls, and restaurants.
